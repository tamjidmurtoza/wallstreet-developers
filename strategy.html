Our Strategy

Our investment strategy focuses on value-add multifamily properties, leveraging opportunities to enhance property value through renovations and improved management. We also undertake heavy value-add projects, such as converting single-family properties into multifamily units and real estate development projects up to seven stories. By targeting well-located properties in growing population density markets, we aim to maximize returns through strategic acquisitions, efficient financing, and proactive asset management.

Strategy:

  • Identify Opportunities: Target well-located, small to mid-size multifamily properties with potential for value enhancement through renovations and better management.
  • Secure Financing: Develop strong relationships with local lenders to secure favorable debt scenarios.
  • Value-Add Execution: Use our construction & civil engineering expertise along with collaboration with local contractors for cost-effective renovations, transforming interiors, and addressing deferred maintenance.
  • Heavy Value-Add: Convert single-family properties to multifamily zoning and develop real estate projects up to seven stories.
  • Asset Management: Maintain active oversight with a dedicated team to ensure financial and operational goals are achieved.
  • Realize Returns: Evaluate scenarios post-renovation to optimize returns through selling, refinancing, or holding.

Acquisition Criteria:

  1. Property Type: Small to mid-size multifamily properties.
  2. Location: Suburban areas near employment hubs, schools, and amenities.
  3. Pricing: $2 million – $20 million.
  4. Property Vintage: 1970s or newer.
  5. Market Focus: Growing markets in New York State.

Benefits:

  1. Stability: Multifamily properties provide stability through economic cycles.
  2. Conversion: Taking advantage of zoning by adding additional units can produce higher returns.
  3. Demand: Rising rental demand due to lifestyle preferences and economic factors.
  4. Returns: Potential for high cash-on-cash yields with proper management and improvements.
